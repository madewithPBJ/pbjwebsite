01 — Identity

Who We Are

PB&J is a white-glove talent agency representing the world's most exceptional surgeons. The brand balances the gravitas of elite medicine with an approachable confidence — luxury without coldness, precision without sterility.

The name is the contrast. Peanut butter & jelly: two powerful things, better together. The warmth of PB. The depth of J. That tension is the brand.

08 — Voice & Tone

How PB&J Speaks

The words are as important as the visuals. PB&J speaks with the confidence of someone who doesn't need to convince you.

We Are

  • Confident, not arrogant
  • Precise, not clinical
  • Warm, not casual
  • Selective, not cold
  • Brief, not terse
  • Authoritative, not intimidating

We Are Not

  • Salesy or pushy
  • Corporate jargon-heavy
  • Overly formal or stiff
  • Vague or abstract
  • Flashy or hyperbolic
  • Generic medical brand voice

Copy Examples

"Intentionally small. Impossibly selective."

"Where expertise meets opportunity."

"We don't represent surgeons. We represent legacies."

"The right surgeon for the right moment."

09 — Do & Don't

Design Rules

Do
  • Use full-viewport sections with generous padding
  • Let whitespace do the heavy lifting
  • Use outlined buttons — never filled gradients
  • Lead sections with a small uppercase eyebrow label
  • Use Cormorant for all emotional/headline copy
  • Shift footer to dark (plum or burgundy)
  • Keep CTAs to one per section
  • Use gold sparingly — it should feel earned
Don't
  • Use stock photos of doctors with stethoscopes
  • Use blue-and-white clinical color combinations
  • Use emojis anywhere — ever
  • Add gradients to buttons
  • Add heavy animations or video backgrounds
  • Cram multiple CTAs into one section
  • Use all-caps for body text
  • Mix icon styles — use one family only
